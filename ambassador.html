#YouTeachBlockchain

The mission of the Blockchain Institute is to educate as many people as possible about the potential of blockchain technology. We created the Blockchain Institute Ambassador Program to enable those that are passionate about blockchain technology to collaborate with us and each other. Help us grow the global blockchain community and spread knowledge about decentralization and cryptocurrencies.

Early Access

Preview our resources before they’re released to the public. Help shape content before it is taught globally. Collaborate with us and other ambassadors across the world.

Contribute

Help create educational resources for others! Break down complex topics into easy to understand FAQs, write about projects not getting enough coverage on our community blog. Let’s work together to share your contribution throughout the community!

Support

We can help you teach and onboard those curious to learn more. Our free resources address common questions that blockchain beginners ask and will provide a deeper understanding of how these technologies work.
