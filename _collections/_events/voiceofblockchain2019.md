<h2>Details</h2>
The Annual Voice of Blockchain conference in Chicago brings together decentralized ledger professionals from across America and around the world. This years' topics include sovereign identity and digital privacy among others.

Our Executive Director, Taylor Gerring, presented a rousing keynote discussing the future of the internet and self-sovereign communication.

<img src="https://theblockchaininstitute.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/20191001_fjs_coincollect_66.jpg" alt="" width="6750" height="4505" />

Our development lead, Alex Morris, participated in a discussion on how digital identity can expand personal freedoms.

<img class="alignnone wp-image-15773 size-full" src="https://theblockchaininstitute.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/20190930_fjs_coincollect_35.jpg" alt="" width="7360" height="4912" />

And finally, our content lead, Jeremy Cogan, provided some insight into technology adoption and our role as an educational organization.
