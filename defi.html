Make Crypto Work For You

DeFi is a term that describes traditional financial services coming to the decentralized cryptocurrency world. For the first time, we have the technology to conduct our own banking services without the use of a third party. This is the vision that DeFi brings to reality. Join us on March 3rd
DeFi can offer a financial system that’s fair and accessible. The Demystifying DeFi, Stablecoins, and NFTs course explores:
  • The road from Bitcoin to Ethereum to DeFi
  • How traditional financial applications are being decentralized
  • The values of the DeFi ecosystem: Openness, Modularity, Decentralization
  • The new financial applications building upon blockchain technology
  • How to navigate the DeFi ecosystem using various tools

The Building Blocks

We didn't get here overnight. Blockchain technology has continued to advance since its inception. This course will teach you about the building blocks that make DeFi possible.

  • DApps: Applications that run on decentralized networks

  • Stablecoins: Cryptocurrency without the volatility

  • Smart Contracts: Autonomous, self-executing programs

  • Non-Fungible Tokens: Representing indivisible assets

Grow
Your Crypto

Join a more inclusive financial system where you have control. Transparent and fair with no centralized systems to take high fees for the services they provide.

  • Open Financial Instruments: No longer reserved just for banks and select investors

  • Yield Farming: A variety of ways to grow your crypto with different levels of risk

  • Liquidity Pools: Provide liquidity with your crypto and earn interest
