Bitcoin addresses are just long strings of numbers and letters. These addresses can be turned into QR codes to make the withdrawal and sending of funds easy. Instead of typing out the full address, you only need to scan the QR code!

Open Wallet

Open your crypto wallet. Select bitcoin and hit the send button.

Find Address

Have the person you want to receive bitcoin open their wallet and select receive. This displays their address as a QR code.

Scan Code

Choose the scan option after you hit send and scan the receiver's QR code.

Send Bitcoin

Enter the amount you want to transfer and hit send.
